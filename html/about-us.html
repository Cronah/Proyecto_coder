About us

We are a reliable score-rating page, we score each value first giving a score ourselves based on our critics and the reviews of different movie critics and we make a score based on that and the fans opinion. that way we provide a movie being rated wrong on purpose. The score is given with roses, 5 being the best score possible and 0 being the worst.

What is the golden rose?

The golden rose is awardes to the best rating movie by far, when a movie positive reviews exceed 90% we award the golden rose to that movie to show how much people liked it.
